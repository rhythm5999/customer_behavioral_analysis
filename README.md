# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes **3,900 customer purchases** to uncover insights into spending patterns, product preferences, subscription behavior, and customer segmentation. The goal is to guide **strategic business decisions** with data-driven recommendations.

---

## 📊 Dataset Summary
- **Rows:** 3,900  
- **Columns:** 18  
- **Features:** Customer demographics, purchase details, shopping behavior  
- **Missing Data:** 37 values in *Review Rating* column  

---

## ⚙️ Data Preparation
1. **Data Loading** – Imported dataset using `pandas`  
2. **Exploration** – Structure check & summary statistics  
3. **Missing Data Handling** – Imputed review ratings with median values  
4. **Feature Engineering** – Created age groups & purchase frequency metrics  
5. **Database Integration** – Connected to **PostgreSQL** for SQL analysis  

---

## 🔎 Key Insights
- **Revenue by Gender:** Female customers generate slightly higher revenue than males.  
- **High-Value Discount Users:** Premium customers maximize discounts while spending above average.  
- **Top-Rated Products:** Blouse, Dress, Shirt consistently receive excellent reviews.  
- **Shipping Preferences:** Express shipping customers spend **12% more** per transaction.  
- **Subscription Impact:** Subscribers spend **68% more**, contribute **45% of revenue**, and show **78% loyalty rate**.  
- **Customer Segmentation:**  
  - Loyal: 15%  
  - Returning: 35%  
  - New: 50%  

---

## 📈 Dashboard
An interactive **Power BI dashboard** was built to visualize:
- Revenue by category & age group  
- Subscription vs non-subscription behavior  
- Shipping type impact  
- Product performance  

---

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations
- **Boost Subscriptions** – Promote exclusive benefits for subscribers  
- **Loyalty Programs** – Reward repeat buyers to increase retention  
- **Targeted Marketing** – Focus on high-revenue segments & express shipping users  
- **Product Positioning** – Highlight top-rated products in campaigns  
- **Discount Policy Review** – Balance margin control with sales boosts  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Python (pandas, matplotlib, seaborn)** – Data cleaning & EDA  
- **PostgreSQL** – Structured business analysis  
- **Power BI** – Interactive dashboards & visualization
